A little about me.

I am Josh Albrecht and welcome to my site. I'm from the St. Louis area right smack dab in the middle of the USA. When I was a child in the 1980s I wanted to be an archeologist. That is until I found out what they really do. Like dig for artifacts with tiny shovels and brooms. What I really wanted to do is run from boulders, jump out of planes, fight Nazis and of course use a big bull whip. So I wanted to be Indiana Jones. I believe it was watching the Great Doctor Jones on the big screen that gave me my first taste of wanderlust.

Flash forward to 2014, I was in my early thirties and had never left the country. My greatest adventures involved me being drunk in public. I medicated my wanderlust with a constant flow of video games and movies. However killing Nazi Zombies on Call of Duty can ony suppress the desire for adventure not cure it. That all changed in March of 2014 when I finally got the balls to travel to Europe. Since then I have tried to leave the country at least once a year. This site is all about my adventures abroad.
